v0.14.0: outer, semi, and anti joins

Implements LEFT OUTER, RIGHT OUTER, FULL OUTER, LEFT SEMI, LEFT ANTI,
RIGHT SEMI, and RIGHT ANTI join variants as an incremental DBSP operator
(`OuterJoinOp`) backed by a batch reference oracle (`OuterJoinOracle`).

Changes:
- crates/rockstream-ops: add `outer_join` module with `OuterJoinOp`,
  `JoinType` enum, unmatched-row zero-crossing state tracking
- crates/rockstream-oracle: extend `join_oracle` with `OuterJoinOracle`,
  `NullCombineFn` type, and `JoinIdx` helper; add `outer_join_tests`
  integration suite (12 tests, 5 proptest property tests)
- Cargo.toml: bump workspace version to 0.14.0
- ROADMAP.md: mark v0.14 ✅ Done
- sign-offs/v0.14.md: full exit-criteria sign-off
