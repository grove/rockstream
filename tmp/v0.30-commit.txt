v0.30.0: Direct exchange with planner-driven combiners

## What

Implements the v0.30 exchange subsystem in `rockstream-runtime::exchange`:

- `ExchangePath` enum (`Elided` / `Loopback` / `Direct` / `Durable`) and
  `ExchangeAnn` planner annotation in `rockstream-types::exchange`
- `ExchangeClassifier`: maps source/target worker identities to an
  `ExchangePath` at plan time (elided → loopback → direct rule tree)
- `LoopbackChannel`: bounded in-process `tokio::mpsc` channel for same-worker
  exchanges; `network_call_count()` is always 0 by construction
- `CreditTracker`: semaphore-based credit backpressure per exchange sender
- `PreShuffleCombiner`: law-generic pre-shuffle merge driven entirely by the
  planner-attached `MergeLawId`; dispatches into the registered `LawBundle`;
  no hard-coded SUM/COUNT/AVG allowlist
- Arrow IPC stream encode/decode helpers (`encode_batch`, `decode_batch`,
  `encoded_size`) using `arrow::ipc::writer::StreamWriter`
