## Notes

- mTLS scaffolding validates PEM files at startup but does not yet perform TLS
  handshakes on the TCP transport. Full TLS handshake integration (via
  `tokio-rustls`) is deferred to v0.46 (Auth/RBAC/mTLS everywhere), consistent
  with the ROADMAP scope.
- The TCP control protocol uses newline-delimited JSON (`\n`-terminated
  `serde_json`). A binary framing protocol (e.g. length-prefixed) will be
  introduced if performance measurements in v0.29/v0.30 show it is needed.
- Tier 1 control service currently runs a no-op pipeline after startup to
  maintain the existing test/audit-trail path. In v0.29 this will be replaced
  by the real shard-leasing loop.
